Privacy Policy

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted partners who help us provide our services, such as tailors or delivery partners, but only as necessary.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us.
