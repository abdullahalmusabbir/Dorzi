Our Story

Founded in 2024, Dozzi emerged from a simple observation: Bangladesh has some of the world's most skilled tailors, yet customers often struggle to find quality craftsmanship that meets their specific needs.

Our platform bridges this gap by connecting customers directly with verified, experienced tailors across the country. We believe that every piece of clothing should tell a story - your story - crafted with precision and care by artisans who understand the art of tailoring.

From traditional panjabis and salwar kameez to modern formal wear, our network of tailors brings decades of experience and cultural knowledge to every stitch. We're not just a marketplace; we're a community that celebrates craftsmanship and preserves the rich heritage of Bengali tailoring.

Our Values
